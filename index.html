Which countries drink the most?

Using data from the World Health Organization, a look at each country's alcohol consumption.

By Nami Sumida
April 2019

Here are the top 20 countries that consume the most alcohol and the number of servings the average person drinks in a year.

Residents of Andorra, a small European country bordering France and Spain, drink the most, averaging 695 servings per year. That's about 2 drinks a day!

And while most other countries in the top 20 are similarly European countries, the type of alcohol residents are drinking vary quite a bit by country.

Some countries primarily drink beer, like Czech Republic and Lithuania. France, unsurprisingly, mostly drinks wine - so does Portugal. And residents of Grenada tend to prefer spirits.

Each dot on the triangle is a country. The closer the dot is to a particular alcoholic drink, the more likely it is for an average person in that country to consume that drink than the others.

The larger the dot, the more alcohol is consumed by a person in that country.

Hover over each dot to see how many servings people in that country consume of each drink.
